Fragmented Transport Management

The Problem

Chemical logistics operators face operational fragmentation: manual route planning, no real-time shipment visibility, time-consuming invoice generation separated by operation type (distribution, containers, isotanks), and difficult Tango software integration for accounting.

The Solution

Chemical logistics companies in Argentina struggle with manual route planning, scattered shipment tracking, complex multi-operation invoicing (distribution, ports, technical cargo), and difficulty integrating billing with accounting systems like Tango.

Target Audience

Logistics managers, operations directors, and billing specialists at chemical transport companies in Argentina

Personal Motivation

Eliminate manual processes, improve fleet utilization, reduce delivery times, and streamline revenue recognition through automated, operation-aware invoicing.
